How the 13-Month, 28-Day Calendar Works

The 13-month calendar divides the year into 13 months, each containing exactly 28 days (4 weeks). This gives a total of 364 days, leaving 1 extra day (or 2 in a leap year) to be added as a "leap day" or an extra holiday at the end of the year.
